\verse{When I was a little lad, ma mamma always told me,
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}
If I didn't kiss the girls ma lips would grow all mouldy.
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}}

\chorus{Way haul away, we'll haul away together,
Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.
Way haul away, we'll haul for better weather,
Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}

\verse{When I was with ma English girl, she was fat and lazy,
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}
Then I met a Spanish girl, she damn near drove me crazy.
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}}

\verse{When I was with ma Irish lass, she was calm and tender,
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}
She left me for a Yankee man, he was so rich and slender.
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}}

\verse{St Patrick drove away the snakes then drank up all the whiskey,
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}
It made him sing and dance a jig, he felt so fine and frisky.
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}}

\verse{King Louis was the king of France before the revolution,
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}
But then he got his head cut off which spoiled his constitution.
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}}

\verse{The cook is in the galley, a-making duff so dandy,
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}
The captain's in his cabin, drinking wine and brandy.
\textit{Way haul away, we'll haul away, Joe.}}
